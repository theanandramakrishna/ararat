# Crossword App: Your Ultimate Cryptic Crossword Companion

## Introduction

Welcome to Crossword App, the premier Android application designed for crossword enthusiasts who crave challenging, cryptic puzzles. Built on the robust Ararat crossword library, this app brings the thrill of solving intricate crosswords right to your fingertips. Whether you're a seasoned solver or just starting your cryptic journey, Crossword App offers an immersive, user-friendly experience that combines classic puzzle-solving with modern cloud-saving capabilities.

With support for the popular .puz file format, Crossword App allows you to load and solve puzzles from various sources, including online archives and puzzle books. The app's intuitive interface, powered by custom crossword rendering, ensures that every clue and grid interaction feels natural and responsive.

## Key Features

### Immersive Crossword Solving
- **High-Quality Rendering**: Experience crisp, scalable crossword grids that adapt to your device's screen. The app uses advanced graphics rendering to display puzzles with precision, including support for circled squares, rebus entries, and themed formatting.
- **Interactive Grid**: Tap cells to input letters, navigate seamlessly with arrow keys or gestures, and enjoy real-time validation. The app highlights selected words and cells, making it easy to track your progress.
- **Clue Navigation**: Access across and down clues in organized lists. Jump directly to any word in the puzzle for efficient solving.
- **Hint System**: Get assistance with "solve cell," "solve word," or "solve entire puzzle" options. Perfect for when you're stuck on a tricky cryptic clue.

### Cloud Integration with Google Drive
- **Seamless Save/Load**: Sign in with your Google account to save your puzzle state directly to Google Drive's app data folder. Your progress is securely stored in the cloud, allowing you to continue solving across devices without losing work.
- **Private Storage**: All data is saved in a dedicated app folder, invisible to other Drive users and apps. No risk of accidental sharing or data exposure.
- **Automatic Sync**: Load your saved state instantly upon opening the app, ensuring a continuous solving experience.

### Puzzle Format Support
- **.puz Compatibility**: Load puzzles in the widely-used .puz format, which supports scrambled puzzles, extra sections for attributes and rebuses, and metadata like titles, authors, and notes.
- **Scrambled Puzzle Unlocking**: For locked puzzles, the app can brute-force unlock them using a 4-digit code, giving you access to premium content.
- **Extensive Metadata**: View puzzle titles, authors, copyrights, and additional notes to enhance your solving context.

### Customization and Accessibility
- **Theme Support**: Enjoy a clean, material design interface that adapts to your device's theme settings.
- **Multidex Support**: Optimized for larger apps, ensuring smooth performance even with extensive puzzle libraries.
- **Input Validation**: Prevent invalid characters and ensure puzzle integrity with built-in validators.
- **Undo Functionality**: Easily revert changes with comprehensive undo support.

### Technical Excellence
- **Kotlin-Powered**: Built with modern Kotlin for reliability, performance, and maintainability.
- **Ararat Library Integration**: Leverages the powerful Ararat crossword engine for accurate parsing, rendering, and state management.
- **Google APIs**: Secure integration with Google Sign-In and Drive APIs for authentication and storage.
- **Open-Source Roots**: Based on the Ararat library, promoting transparency and community contributions.

## How to Use Crossword App

### Getting Started
1. **Download and Install**: Get Crossword App from the Google Play Store (or sideload the APK for testing).
2. **Load a Puzzle**: Import a .puz file from your device storage or online sources.
3. **Start Solving**: Tap the grid to begin entering letters. Use the clue lists to navigate and reference hints.

### Saving Your Progress
- Tap the menu and select "Save to Drive" after signing in with Google.
- Your state is automatically saved to the app's private Drive folder.

### Advanced Features
- **Rebus Support**: Handle multi-character entries with ease.
- **Attribute Awareness**: Recognize circled squares and no-solution cells for accurate solving.
- **Scrambled Puzzles**: Unlock and solve password-protected puzzles.
